An end-to-end data analytics project focused on optimizing supply chain operations, forecasting demand, and managing inventory efficiently.

## üìä Project Overview
This notebook processes the Slooze Challenge dataset to extract actionable business insights across five key domains:
1. **Demand Forecasting:** Utilized Meta's Prophet library for time-series forecasting to predict future 30-day product demand.
2. **ABC Analysis:** Applied the Pareto Principle to classify inventory into high (A), moderate (B), and low (C) priority tiers.
3. **Economic Order Quantity (EOQ):** Calculated the optimal order sizes to minimize holding and ordering costs.
4. **Reorder Point (ROP) Analysis:** Determined exact inventory thresholds for reordering, factoring in safety stock and lead times.
5. **Lead Time Analysis:** Assessed supplier efficiency by analyzing procurement timelines.
